About IV Drips

With increasingly busier lifestyles and decreasing practice of cooking from scratch with fresh and organic ingredients, studies have shown that there is a disturbing depletion in nutrient absorption through our everyday diets.

Unlike oral supplementation, IV administration of nutrients can bypass gastrointestinal absorption saturation and increased renal clearance, resulting in high serum concentrations not obtainable with oral or even intramuscular administration.

WHY USE AN IV DRIP?

While undergoing an IV Vitamin Drip, your body receives a higher concentration of vitamins than when consumed the oral way.

A vitamin that is taken orally gets broken down in the stomach and digestive tract, meaning that the amount absorbed by the body is significantly less (50%), than when through an IV (90%).

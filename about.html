ABOUT OUR CLINIC

Aesthetic by Maria has a reputation that truly speaks for itself. Led by Maria Fernanda, one of the most highly regarded professionals in the industry, our clinic offers top quality treatments carried out by a team of experienced medics and biomedics.

From the renowned Morpheus 8 and Lumeca to Forma, we only use the most advanced technologies and the best products on the market, ensuring our clients receive nothing but the finest. At Aesthetic by Maria, we are proud to bring the essence of Brazilian Beauty right to the heart of London. Brazilian Beauty is renowned for its natural, radiant, sculpted and vibrant glow.

This unique aesthetic celebrates the diversity of beauty and the harmony of features, and we are thrilled to offer it to our clients here in the UK. This cultural heritage means we are experts in catering to the full spectrum of beauty, no matter the complexion or skin texture. Our team is skilled in using advanced techniques that enhance and complement each client's natural beauty, tailored specifically to their unique skin needs.

By combining our deep knowledge of skin diversity with cutting-edge technologies, we ensure that every treatment delivers effective and long-lasting results. Let us elevate your natural radiance with treatments designed to bring out your best, all in a setting of pure elegance and care. Come and discover the unique experience that awaits you!

Dr. Paul Tulley

Paul Tulley is a Consultant Plastic and Reconstructive Surgeon based in London and the South-East of the UK. He specialises in all aspects of cosmetic surgery for female and male patients including facial rejuvenation surgery, rhinoplasty, cosmetic breast surgery, abdominoplasty and body contouring.

He also specialises in reconstructive surgery for facial palsy, skin cancer and the abdominal wall.

He utilises the latest plastic surgery techniques and his aesthetic skill to produce exceptional, natural results, in the safe environment of the highest quality hospitals in and around London.

Paul is a member of leading plastic surgery associations, including the British Association of Aesthetic Plastic Surgeons (BAAPS) and the American Society of Plastic Surgeons (ASPS).

Dr. Camila Azzini

I am Dr. Camila Azzini, dermatologist graduated more than 20 years ago from the Rio de Janeiro State University with post-graduation in clinical – surgical dermatology and cosmiatrics.

I am passionate about the study of skin and hair and their relationship to holistic health. I am a full member of the SBD (Brazilian Society of Dermatology), the AAD (American Academy of Dermatology), and the Brazilian Society of Dermatologic Surgery.

Today I divide my time between studies, face-to-face appointments in London – UK, online appointments and share my love for dermatology and life on my Youtube and Instagram channels.

Dr. Bianca

Dr Bianca is a fully qualified General Practitioner with extensive experience in Aesthetic Medicine. She previously trained as a General surgeon in Brazil and as a General Practitioner in the NHS. Her passion is to help patients excel their true beauty.

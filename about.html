About Maria

Skin health and care is an important part of any woman’s wellbeing. Maria’s journey with her skin health was the catalyst to her becoming a qualified professional.

“As a young girl, I never had any trouble with my skin. This is why it came as such a surprise when acne presented itself in my mid-twenties. Not only that, but throughout my pregnancy, I also developed melasma. As you can imagine, this was a stressful time.

I went to countless specialists, which cost me thousands of pounds, and could never find a solution. Until one day, whilst visiting my hometown in Brazil, a Dermatologist found the solution in a simple and inexpensive face cream. This was baffling to me because previous specialists had always tried to sell me expensive and sometimes damaging treatments that never worked.

My journey ultimately motivated me to help women who are in the same position as I was to find simple, yet effective, solutions to feeling radiant, healthy and beautiful.

If you have a similar story and you’re looking for specialist advice, I’d love to help you find a solution that fits your budget and helps you feel like the best version of yourself.”

Maria Fernanda Smith

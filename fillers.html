About Fillers

Dermal fillers can be used to improve the appearance in a variety of ways and can be applied to specific points on the face, depending on the concern that needs to be addressed. A versatile treatment that can be administered to the mid and lower parts of the face, dermal fillers can help to restore a youthfully rejuvenated look, or to enhance the natural contouring and definition of the features. The injectable gel also contains a high concentration of hyaluronic acid, flooding the skin with moisture to boost hydration in the skin.

About Booster Shot Injections

We can get our vitamins through food or oral supplements, but there’s actually an even better way to get them: booster shots. Fast and effective, booster shots (vitamin injections) deliver a powerful, concentrated dose of vitamins to improve your health, well-being, and appearance. Vitamin injections make an effective addition to your regular health and/or beauty regimen.

AREAS OF TREATMENT

Vitamin injections are highly effective because they are administered via shot or vitamin drip IV therapy, which means the vitamins avoid going through your GI metabolism, making them more bioavailable. In addition, vitamin injections and vitamin drips are of a higher quality than oral vitamins because tablets are formulated with binders, fillers, and other materials. This reduces the quality of oral vitamins considerably. With vitamin booster shots, you will experience better, more significant results in less time because you’re giving your body the nutrients it’s craving.

Dermal Fillers

About Dermal Fillers

Dermal fillers are substances injected into your face with the purpose of filling lines and wrinkles and adding volume to areas such as your lips cheeks, chin, etc. Fillers are non-permanent and last between 6 and 18 months. This vastly depends on the type of filler and which area it is injected.

Most dermal fillers used in the UK are made up of a natural substance called hyaluronic acid.

HYALURONIC ACID

Hyaluronic Acid (HA) is a clear, viscous liquid and a natural component of skin, bones, cartilage and connective tissue.

The most important property of HA is its high water-binding (Hydro-phallic) capacity. This gives the skin is moisture, elasticity and resilience. As we age, the skin's Hyaluronic acid content decreases making it less elastic, resulting in visible lines and wrinkles.

Benefits of Dermal Fillers

  • Adds volume to the skin
  • Can remove lines and wrinkles
  • Shapes, contours and adds volume to the face
  • Improve and increase skin elasticity
  • Immediate results and no downtime
  • Long-lasting effects up to 18 months (Perfectha)
  • Self-confidence builder
  • Boost your collagen

PDO Threads

About PDO Threads

The PDO (Polydioxanone) Thread Lift and Smoothing procedure is the use of absorbable surgical sutures placed into the subdermal layer of the skin to help tighten and lift your skin.

The procedure is a less invasive face-lift and can often be performed in under 45-minutes, depending on the patient’s initial situation.

PDO thread lifts use a biodegradable suture and are best suited for rejuvenating the skin, whilst newer types of thread lifts are better at lifting sagging skin.

Patients can see immediate improvements in skin sagging after undergoing the procedure, and results can last between 6 months and 3 years, depending on the patient’s age, skin volume and elasticity.

AREAS OF TREATMENT

PDO threads can be used to treat most areas of the face that are experiencing signs of ageing. The most commonly treated areas are:

  • Cheeks
  • Jaw
  • Neck
  • Eyes
